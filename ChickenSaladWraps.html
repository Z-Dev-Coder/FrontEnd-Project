Servings: 4
Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 0 mins

  • 1 cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced tomatoes

Chicken Salad Wraps

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, mayonnaise, celery, onion, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well until thoroughly combined.
  2. Place a lettuce leaf on each tortilla.
  3. Spoon the chicken salad mixture evenly onto the lettuce leaves.
  4. Top with sliced cucumbers and tomatoes.
  5. Roll up the tortillas tightly and secure with toothpicks.
  6. Cut each wrap in half diagonally and serve immediately.
