Keep In Touch With Us

(FAQ)

1. What is "In a Box"?

In a Box is a home food delivery service that provides weekly recipe guides, bake-at-home sets, and high-quality ingredients, helping you prepare delicious meals in the comfort of your kitchen. We also offer custom packages to cater to your dietary preferences and allergies.

2. How do I place an order?

You can easily place an order through our Order Now page. Simply select the package that best suits your needs, and we’ll take care of the rest! If you have special requests or want a custom package, you can contact us for further assistance.

3. What’s included in each package?

Each package varies based on your selection: Beginners: 15 weekly recipes, 1 bake-at-home set, discounts on our app and gift boxes. Everyday: 35 weekly recipes, 3 bake-at-home sets, 50% off our nutritional app, and 1 free gift box. Deluxe: 50 weekly recipes, 5 bake-at-home sets, free access to our app, and 3 gift boxes. Custom Packages: Tailored to dietary restrictions or allergies, available upon request.

4. Can I customize my package?

Yes! We understand that dietary needs vary, so we offer custom packages. To create a custom box, please contact us through our Contact Us page, and we’ll work with you to design a package that suits your preferences.

5. How do I contact customer service?

You can reach us via phone, email, or social media. Visit our Contact Us page for all the details. We’re here to help with any questions or concerns.
